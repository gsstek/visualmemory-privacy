Privacy Policy for Visual Memory

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

Visual Memory is a photo memory app. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data we collect

Visual Memory stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal data with third parties.

The app stores:

  • Photos you take or import
  • Notes and tags you add to memories
  • App settings and preferences

All data is stored only on your device and never leaves it unless you explicitly export a backup file.

Camera and photo library access

The app requires camera access to take photos. We only access your camera when you actively use the camera feature. Photos are stored locally on your device.

Biometric data

If you enable biometric lock (Premium feature), the app uses your device's built-in authentication system. We do not access, store, or transmit any biometric data.

In-app purchases

Premium purchases are processed by Google Play. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Third-party services

We do not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page.
